About

Data-driven.
Context-obsessed.

Originally from Irpinia and now based in Geneva, I am a financial analyst with three years of international experience at Ralph Lauren Europe. I have worked across the full planning cycle — budgeting, forecasting, variance analysis, KPI reporting — covering EMEA and APAC markets and delivering to regional CEOs, CFOs, and Managing Directors.

Educated at Bocconi University in Economics & Management, I recently completed the Financial Planning & Analysis Professional (FPAP) certification from the Corporate Finance Institute — a formal consolidation of what I had already been building in practice.

Outside of finance, I run Borderliner, an editorial newsletter on economics, history, and culture. I am also a certified sommelier — a discipline that, like financial analysis, demands precision, context, and the ability to read what is not immediately visible.
