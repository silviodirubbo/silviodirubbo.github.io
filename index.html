About

I am a Business Analyst with three years of experience in data analysis, reporting, and digital operations across EMEA and LATAM. I graduated from Bocconi University with a Master’s degree in Economics and Management for Arts, Culture, Media, and Entertainment (110/110). My work combines analytical thinking with curiosity for culture, storytelling, and design.

I am interested in how systems work — whether they are markets, organisations, or societies — and how people interpret them. This is what connects my professional projects, my newsletter Borderliner, and my writing about cinema.
