Applied FP&A / BI Project

Meridian
Time Group

Meridian Time Group is a fictional multi-collection consumer goods manufacturer, built to practise FP&A and BI work end-to-end: ten product collections spanning Entry, Mid, and High price tiers, sold through wholesale and boutique channels with an EMEA and LATAM focus.

The scenario runs on a purpose-built dataset (three years of sell-in, sell-out, budget, cost, and working-capital data) so every model, dashboard, and report below is built against real transactional structure rather than static screenshots.

Looking for quick, standalone report samples instead? See the Data Visualisation section on the CV.
